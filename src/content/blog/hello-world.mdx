## On starting fresh

Sometimes you need to tear something down to build it back up. The old site served its purpose, but I wanted something simpler — a place to write and think out loud.

> The best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago. The second best time is now.

There's something liberating about a blank page. No legacy constraints, no accumulated cruft. Just words and ideas.
